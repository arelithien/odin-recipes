Chewy Chocolate Chip Recipe



DESCRIPTION

I have a few tricks that make these the best soft chocolate chip cookies that you’ll ever try. With hundreds of positive reviews from bakers around the world, I’m confident you’ll fall in love with this chocolate chip cookie recipe too. Chilling the cookie dough is imperative and cornstarch makes them extra soft and thick!

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups + 2 tbsp All purpose Flour
  • 3/4 cups of cocoa powder
  • 1 tspn baking soda
  • 1/2 tspn salt
  • 2 1/2 sticks or 1 1/4 cup of butter room temperature
  • 2 cups of granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tspn vanilla
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips, kisses, or chips of choice (placed in the center after baking)


DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients and set them aside.
  3. Electric mix butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla mix until just combined.
  5. Slowly add dry to wet ingredients until combined
  6. Refrigerate for an hour.
  7. Bake the cookies for 10-12 minutes
