Rotisserie Chicken Recipe



DESCRIPTION

Nothing beats an easy to make and even easier to prepare roast chicken! Especially when roasted in a delicious seasoning like Rustic Rub. This Rustic, Juicy Rotisserie Chicken will make everyone come back for seconds!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 cup Rustic Rub
  • One 4-pound whole chicken, rinsed


DIRECTIONS

  1. Rub the chicken with the 1/4 cup Rustic Rub.
  2. Truss the chicken. Attach one of the Rotisserie Forks to the Rotisserie Spit and tighten the screws on the Fork. Slide the chicken on the spit rod into the secured spit fork. Secure the chicken on the Rotisserie Spit with the other Rotisserie Fork and screws.
  3. Insert the chicken into the Rotisserie connections inside the Power AirFryer 360.
  4. Rotate the Program Selection Knob to the Rotisserie setting. Rotate the Temperature Control Knob to 350 degrees F/177 degrees C and the Time Control Knob to 55 minutes. Press the Start/Pause Button to begin the cooking cycle.
  5. When the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 160 degrees F/71 degrees C, the chicken is ready to serve. Press the Cancel Button and remove the chicken from the Power AirFryer 360. Let the chicken rest for 15 minutes before serving.
